Boosting from Your LinkedIn Page

After you've established your LinkedIn Page and are engaging regularly with your community, it's time to invest in growing your brand and business beyond the borders of your followers.  One simple way to get started with advertising is to use Boosting to quickly turn an organic post into a paid ad.

When you start a Boost, LinkedIn automatically creates a Campaign Manager ad account for you. This makes it easy for you to evolve your ad campaign strategy beyond Boosting, when you're ready.

With Boosting, you are able to promote several types of content: text posts, single images, events, and videos. To create campaigns with other ad types such as carousel ads or dynamic ads, you will want to use Campaign Manager. Using Campaign Manager also unlocks more sophisticated bidding and advanced audience targeting.

Let's take a look at how to get started in Campaign Manager!
