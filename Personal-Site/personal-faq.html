Frequently Asked Questions

Our mall is located at in the center of the city some few kilometers away from the stadium. You can get here by your private car, bicycle or via public transportation options, like bus, taxi or train lines. We have ample parking space available, including free parking for the first three hours.

The mall is open from 10 am to 9 pm from Monday to Friday, 10 am to 10 pm on Saturdays, and 11 am to 7 pm on Sundays. Operating hours may vary on holidays, so please check our website for specific dates.

- We offer a variety of dining options in our food court, open during mall hours. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the mall. There are children's play areas on the second floor and family restrooms with diaper-changing facilities.

You can check our website or app for information on current events and promotions. We also run a loyalty program that provides members with exclusive discounts and early access to sales events.

Our customer service desk is located in the central atrium on the ground floor. You can also reach mall management at +1(289) 555 701 or heybrookshopping65@outlook.com.

The mall's general policy allows returns within 30 days of purchase with a receipt. However, individual stores may have their own return policies, so we recommend checking with each store for specific details. Mall gift cards are available for purchase at the customer service desk or online. They can be used in all stores within the mall that accept them. Please inquire at individual stores to confirm acceptance.

Restrooms are located on every floor and are cleaned frequently for your comfort and safety. If you lose an item during your visit, please report it to our lost and found service located at the customer service desk.
